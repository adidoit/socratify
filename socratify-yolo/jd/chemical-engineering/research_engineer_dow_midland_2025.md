# Research & Development Engineer - Dow Chemical
**Location:** Midland, MI  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time  
**Posted Date:** September 2025  
**Application Deadline:** November 15, 2025  

## Company Overview
Dow combines the power of science and technology to passionately innovate what is essential to human progress. Our scientists and engineers are among the most advanced in the industry, working at the intersection of chemistry, physics, and engineering to develop breakthrough materials.

## Position Summary
Join Dow's Corporate Research organization in Midland as a Research & Development Engineer. You will work on cutting-edge materials science research, developing new products and processes that will drive Dow's future growth and contribute to solving global challenges.

## Key Responsibilities
- Conduct fundamental and applied research in polymer science and chemical engineering
- Design and execute laboratory experiments to develop new materials and processes
- Utilize advanced characterization techniques to understand structure-property relationships
- Collaborate with business teams to translate research into commercial applications
- Support scale-up activities from laboratory to pilot and commercial scale
- Present research findings to internal stakeholders and external conferences
- Participate in patent preparation and intellectual property development
- Mentor students and junior researchers
- Collaborate with external partners including universities and technology providers

## Required Qualifications
- PhD in Chemical Engineering, Materials Science, Chemistry, or related field
- Strong background in polymer science or materials chemistry
- Experience with laboratory research and experimental design
- Knowledge of characterization techniques (NMR, GPC, DSC, TGA, etc.)
- Proficiency in data analysis and statistical methods
- Excellent scientific writing and presentation skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of multidisciplinary teams

## Preferred Qualifications
- Post-doctoral research experience
- Experience with process development and scale-up
- Knowledge of polymerization kinetics and mechanisms
- Familiarity with computational chemistry or molecular modeling
- Experience with pilot plant operations
- Publication record in peer-reviewed journals
- Industry research experience

## Research Focus Areas
- **Circular Economy:** Recycling technologies and sustainable materials
- **Electronics Materials:** Advanced materials for semiconductor applications
- **Packaging Innovation:** Next-generation packaging solutions
- **Energy Storage:** Materials for batteries and energy applications
- **Catalysis:** New catalytic systems for improved processes
