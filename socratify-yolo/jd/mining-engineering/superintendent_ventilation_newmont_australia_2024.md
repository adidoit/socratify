## Position Overview
Superintendent Ventilation position at Newmont's Cadia Valley Operations in Orange, New South Wales. This senior-level role oversees all aspects of mine ventilation systems for underground and surface operations, ensuring optimal air quality, temperature control, and regulatory compliance while supporting production targets.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead and manage the mine ventilation engineering team
- Design and optimize ventilation systems for underground mining operations
- Develop ventilation strategies for new mining areas and expansion projects
- Monitor and analyze ventilation performance data and air quality measurements
- Ensure compliance with Australian mining ventilation regulations
- Manage ventilation equipment maintenance and upgrade programs
- Coordinate with mining operations on ventilation requirements
- Implement energy-efficient ventilation solutions and cost optimization
- Conduct hazard identification and risk assessment for ventilation systems
- Prepare technical reports and presentations for senior management

- Excellent project management and organizational skills
