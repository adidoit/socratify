**Location:** Remote (Global) or New York, NY

## About ConsenSys
ConsenSys is the leading Ethereum software company, enabling developers, enterprises, and people worldwide to build next-generation applications and access the decentralized web. With MetaMask serving over 30 million monthly users and Infura powering thousands of applications, we're at the forefront of Web3 adoption.

## Job Description
We are seeking a Senior Business Development Manager to drive strategic partnerships and enterprise adoption of ConsenSys products. You'll work with Fortune 500 companies, Web3 projects, and ecosystem partners to accelerate the adoption of Ethereum and decentralized technologies.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and execute partnership strategies for MetaMask, Infura, and ConsenSys ecosystem
- Lead enterprise sales cycles for blockchain infrastructure and Web3 solutions
- Build relationships with key decision makers at major corporations and Web3 projects
- Negotiate complex partnership agreements and integration deals
- Collaborate with product teams to define enterprise feature requirements
- Represent ConsenSys at industry conferences and networking events
- Analyze market trends and competitive landscape in Web3 space
- Support go-to-market strategies for new product launches
- Manage strategic accounts and ensure successful partnership execution

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, or related field
- 5+ years of business development experience in technology or financial services
- Strong understanding of blockchain technology and Web3 ecosystem
- Proven track record of closing complex B2B partnerships and deals
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Experience with CRM systems and sales pipeline management
- Ability to work independently in fast-paced, remote environment
- Willingness to travel for client meetings and industry events

## Preferred Qualifications
- Previous experience in cryptocurrency or blockchain companies
- MBA or advanced degree in business or technology
- Network of contacts in Web3, DeFi, or enterprise technology
- Experience with enterprise software sales or fintech partnerships
- Understanding of developer tools and infrastructure products
- Multilingual capabilities for global business development
