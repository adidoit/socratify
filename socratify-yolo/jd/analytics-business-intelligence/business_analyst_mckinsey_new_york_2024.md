## Company Overview

McKinsey & Company is a global management consulting firm that serves leading businesses, governments, non-governmental organizations, and not-for-profits. We help our clients make lasting improvements to their performance and realize their most important goals.

## Position Summary

As a Business Analyst at McKinsey, you will play an active role in many aspects of client engagements, including fact gathering, analysis, client communications, making recommendations, and implementation support. You will collaborate with senior consultants, client managers, and executives on every part of the consulting process, from brainstorming ideas to working side-by-side with clients to implement team recommendations.

## Key Responsibilities

- Conduct quantitative and qualitative analysis to support client recommendations across various industries
- Develop financial models, market sizing analyses, and competitive assessments
- Design and execute primary research including surveys, interviews, and focus groups
- Create compelling presentations for C-level executives and board members
- Support implementation of strategic initiatives and transformation programs
- Analyze large datasets to identify patterns, trends, and actionable insights
- Build and maintain client relationships through professional excellence and thought leadership
- Collaborate with global teams on complex, multi-workstream engagements
- Mentor junior team members and contribute to knowledge development initiatives
