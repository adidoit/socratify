## Role Overview
The Product Manager for Growth will work closely with cross-functional teammates across design, engineering, data science, research, customer experience and product marketing to define, and execute on, a vision for scaling consumer-facing products at Notion. This role involves building features that drive user acquisition, activation, retention, and expansion across Notion's rapidly growing user base.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Growth Strategy Development:** Define and execute comprehensive growth strategies across the entire user funnel from acquisition to retention and monetization
- **Cross-functional Leadership:** Lead collaboration with design, engineering, data science, research, customer experience, and product marketing teams
- **User Journey Optimization:** Improve onboarding flows, feature discovery, and engagement mechanisms that drive long-term user value
- **Data-Driven Experimentation:** Design and execute A/B tests to validate growth hypotheses and optimize user experiences
- **Consumer Product Focus:** Build features specifically designed for individual users and small teams to drive viral growth
- **Global Scaling:** Ensure growth strategies work effectively across different markets and user segments
- **Product-Led Growth:** Develop features that encourage organic user acquisition and word-of-mouth marketing
