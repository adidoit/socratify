## About Stripe
Stripe builds economic infrastructure for the internet. Businesses of every size use our software to accept payments and manage their operations online. Our Customer Success team ensures customers can quickly and successfully integrate Stripe into their business operations.

## Position Overview
We're seeking a Customer Onboarding Specialist to join our growing Customer Success team. You'll be responsible for guiding new customers through their initial Stripe setup and integration, ensuring they achieve value quickly and efficiently while building the foundation for long-term success.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead onboarding for 40-60 new customers per month across various business sizes and industries
- Conduct discovery calls to understand customer business models and payment requirements
- Design customized onboarding plans based on customer technical complexity and timeline
- Guide customers through Stripe Dashboard setup, API integration, and testing processes
- Coordinate with Technical Account Managers and Support teams for complex integrations
- Deliver product demonstrations and training sessions for customer teams
- Monitor onboarding progress and proactively address blockers or delays
- Create and maintain onboarding documentation and best practices
- Collect feedback to improve onboarding processes and customer experience
- Achieve onboarding completion targets and time-to-value metrics

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in customer onboarding, implementation, or technical support
- Strong understanding of APIs, webhooks, and payment processing concepts
- Experience with SaaS platforms and technical product implementations
- Excellent communication skills for both technical and non-technical audiences
- Proven ability to manage multiple projects and priorities simultaneously
- Customer-focused mindset with strong problem-solving abilities
- Comfort working in a fast-paced, high-growth environment

## Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with payments, fintech, or e-commerce industries
- Technical background or experience with developer tools
- Previous experience with Stripe or similar payment platforms
- Knowledge of programming languages (JavaScript, Python, Ruby, etc.)
- Project management certification or experience
- Multilingual capabilities (Spanish, French, or Portuguese)
