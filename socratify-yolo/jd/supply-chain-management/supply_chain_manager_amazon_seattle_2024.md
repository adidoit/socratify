## Position Overview
**Job Title:** Supply Chain Manager I  
**Location:** Seattle, Washington  
**Employment Type:** Full-time  
**Department:** Amazon Global Logistics - North America Destination Operations  
**Reports To:** Senior Supply Chain Manager  

Amazon's North America Destination Operations team is hiring a Supply Chain Manager I to drive strategic initiatives and process improvements across our global logistics network. This role will own supply chain planning at the highest level for Amazon's fulfillment business, with a holistic view of supply and demand within the Amazon fulfillment network.

## Key Responsibilities
- Drive strategic initiatives to optimize network capacity across 200+ fulfillment centers
- Analyze large datasets to identify customer experience improvements and cost reduction opportunities
- Manage relationships with logistics service providers and negotiate service level agreements
- Implement large scale strategic initiatives in inventory optimization and transportation cost management
- Own mid to long term topology strategy and build plan management for delivery stations
- Develop and execute supply chain contingency plans for peak seasons and disruptions
- Lead cross-functional projects with engineering, operations, and finance teams
- Utilize machine learning and AI to improve demand forecasting accuracy
- Support Amazon's sustainability initiatives including carbon-neutral delivery programs
- Mentor junior supply chain analysts and coordinate with global teams

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management, Engineering, Business, or related field
- 3+ years of program or project management experience
- 2+ years of supply chain experience, preferably in e-commerce or retail
- Advanced knowledge of Excel and SQL for data analysis
- Experience with statistical analysis and data modeling
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in fast-paced environment
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience managing multiple projects simultaneously

## Preferred Qualifications
- Master's degree in Supply Chain Management, Operations, Engineering, Analytics or related field
- Experience with AWS cloud services and big data analytics
- Knowledge of machine learning applications in supply chain
- Experience with Lean Six Sigma methodologies
- Background in e-commerce fulfillment operations
- Certification in APICS SCOR or similar supply chain credentials
- Experience with international logistics and customs processes

## Technical Skills Required
